Voice Testing
Check On Portal
For every test case in the playbook, run it twice: once by typing, once by speaking. This tells us whether text and voice produce the same results.
01 Open the EnPro portal. Look for a microphone button or voice icon in the chat interface.
02 If you see it — click it, speak the test input clearly, and note what comes back. Mark the voice result separately from the typed result.
03 If you don't see a mic button — voice is not yet wired in. Skip voice for now and test text only. Note "Voice: Not Available" in your findings.
04 Speak naturally and at normal speed. Note if the AI misheard anything or if part numbers came through garbled. That's important data.
🚨 When to Stop and Call Peter

If Smoke Tests S-01 to S-05 fail, the portal is down, or the AI is making up products that don't exist. Message Peter on Signal immediately.

📸 How to Screenshot

Windows: Win + Shift + S — drag to select the area. Paste it into your bug report. Always screenshot a FAIL.

⚠️ The Most Critical Tests

P-06 and C-03 — the AI must NEVER make up a product or chemical. If it does, that is a BLOCKING failure. Flag it immediately.

⏱️ How Long Will This Take

Full test suite: 60–90 minutes. Short version (priority tests only): 30 minutes. You don't have to do it all at once.
